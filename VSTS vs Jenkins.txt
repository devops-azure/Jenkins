

Azure Pipelines offers free CI/CD with unlimited minutes and 10 parallel jobs for every open source project.

Many of the top open source projects are already using Azure Pipelines for CI/CD, including Atom, Cpython, Pipenv, Tox, Visual Studio Code, and TypeScript.


As for pricing, Azure DevOps is free for open source projects and small projects (up to five users). 
For larger teams, the cost ranges from $30 per month (10 users) to $6,150 per month (1,000 users).
